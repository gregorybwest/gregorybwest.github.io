Actualize Capstone Project

The culmination of my learnings at Actualize: Local Gigs . A place where concertgoers can find all the music events happening locally on a map, and music artists can search for venues and post their upcoming local shows, as well have their own artist profiles where potential fans can hear their music through an embedded Spotify link. The app utilizes a Rails backend framework, persists the data using PostgreSQL , a Vue.js frontend, as well as muliple API endpoints and the Mapbox library. I'm excited to continue working on this project and to add more features.
